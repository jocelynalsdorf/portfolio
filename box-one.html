Harold the Movie Picking Cat

My First API App

Meet Harold. He was my co-pilot as I hacked up my first API app. It uses the IMDB API. Harold will generate a list of movies for you based on the year you were born (or any year you input) and whether you prefer cats or dogs. Harold is grumpy though, and if you choose dogs over cats the movies he picks for you may not be of the highest caliber.
